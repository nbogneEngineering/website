Common Questions

What stage is nBogne at?

We have a working prototype with validated routing and format conversion. We're now building our production sandbox and seeking first pilots with EMR vendors and hospital networks.

What does it cost?

Pricing depends on your setup — EMR integrations, transaction volume, support level. We're flexible during pilot phase. Reach out and we'll discuss what makes sense for you.

How long does integration take?

For EMRs with standard APIs, days to weeks. For complex legacy systems, we work with you on custom adapters. Either way, faster than building interoperability yourself.

What formats do you support?

HL7v2 (2.3, 2.5, 2.7), FHIR R4, JSON/REST, CSV, and XML. If you have something else, let's talk — our format library keeps growing.

Can we pilot first?

Absolutely. We prefer pilots. Prove it works for your environment before committing. That's how trust gets built — through demonstration, not promises.

Where do you operate?

Starting in Ghana, with plans for Kenya and Rwanda. If you're elsewhere in Africa, still reach out — we're always interested in the right partnerships.
