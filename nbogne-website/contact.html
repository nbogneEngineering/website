FAQ

Common Questions

What stage is nBogne at?

We are at the prototype stage. Our core compression & transmission technology has been validated, and we're preparing for field pilots with health facilities in Ghana.

What does the partnership model look like?

We partner through a B2G / B2B2G model — working with Ministries of Health, NGOs, and digital-health integrators. Pricing is per-facility per-month to keep it predictable.

How long does integration take?

Our lightweight REST API means most HMIS and EMR systems can integrate within 2–4 weeks. We provide hands-on support throughout.

Can nBogne handle medical images or lab data?

Currently we focus on structured text-based records (DHIS2 forms, patient registries, lab results as text). Medical imaging support is on our roadmap for 2026+.

Can we run a pilot in our country?

Yes! We're actively looking for partners in Sub-Saharan Africa to run field pilots. Reach out via the form above or book a call to discuss scope and timelines.

Where does nBogne currently operate?

Our team is based in Accra, Ghana, with advisory connections in the US and across West Africa. We're building for all of Sub-Saharan Africa and the Global South.
