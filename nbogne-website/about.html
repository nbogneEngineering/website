About nBogne

"nBogne" means "they have received it" in Twi. It's the confirmation that data arrived where it needed to go.

Our Story

Built from real frustration

We've watched health workers re-enter data after 3-day internet outages. We've seen ministries go blind during outbreaks because systems couldn't sync. We've heard vendors lose bids because they couldn't guarantee offline operation.

The infrastructure exists — 94% cellular coverage, 70+ countries on DHIS2. What's missing is the transport layer that works when internet doesn't.

We built nBogne because the Global South deserves infrastructure that actually works.

Our Mission

To make data flow reliably across connectivity-challenged environments — starting with health, then expanding to every sector that needs it.

Our Vision

Health is the first vertical. The same problem exists across law enforcement, voter registers, national ID, and e-governance. We're building the transport backbone for the Global South.

Why Partners Win

We're not competing with you

EMR vendors don't want to build transport layers. Hospital networks don't want to manage carrier relationships. Ministries don't want to maintain connectivity infrastructure.

We handle the hard part so you can focus on your core business. Bundle nBogne with your solution, win more bids, and let us worry about keeping data flowing.

Your EHR + nBogne = Competitive advantage in connectivity-challenged markets.
